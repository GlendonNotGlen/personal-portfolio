Hack For Good 2022

Hack For Good is a student-run annual hackathon which seeks use technology for social causes. My team and I was shortlisted as a finalist among 40 other teams for our scalable and low cost Web App, streamlining the matching between Social Enterprises and Consultants.

The Demo website was made using Bootstrap, Firebase Authentication, and Firestore Database.
